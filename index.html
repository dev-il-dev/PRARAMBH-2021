ABOUT PRARAMBH-2021

Hey there people !!

Yes you all who are reading this right now, who else would it be? Anyways, we have got news for you. Do you feel like your creative enzymes are all fired up and constantly bombarding your mind with ideas? Well, then you never know, it might just be an entrepreneur inside of you itching to come out. So buckle up, Team EDC, IIEST Shipur is here with ‘ Prarambh: Ideation to Execution ’,a boot camp organized in association with National Entrepreneur Network(Wadhwani Foundation), where you will be turned from a Idea to prototype/MVP stage/ Market Launch Ready Stage.

This Bootcamp will be a structured program where the participants will first go through a stern selection process. The short-listed students would be then taken through a series of structured sessions/interventions, booster clinics, milestone-based journeys to refine product-market fit, understand customer acquisition strategies and iteratively implement venture growth strategies.
