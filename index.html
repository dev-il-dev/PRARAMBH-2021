ABOUT PRARAMBH-2021

Hey there people !!

Yes you all who are reading this right now, who else would it be? Anyways, we have got news for you. Do you feel like your creative enzymes are all fired up and constantly bombarding your mind with ideas? Well, then you never know, it might just be an entrepreneur inside of you itching to come out. So buckle up, Team EDC, IIEST Shipur is here with ‘ Prarambh: Ideation to Execution ’,a boot camp organized in association with National Entrepreneur Network(Wadhwani Foundation), where you will be turned from a Idea to prototype/MVP stage/ Market Launch Ready Stage.

This Bootcamp will be a structured program where the participants will first go through a stern selection process. The short-listed students would be then taken through a series of structured sessions/interventions, booster clinics, milestone-based journeys to refine product-market fit, understand customer acquisition strategies and iteratively implement venture growth strategies.

FAQ

What is Prarambh Bootcamp?

        Prarambh is an Entrepreneurship Bootcamp, which will help to transform your idea from Ideation stage to Execution stage.

What will be the structure of event?

        It will be held online. you will get mentorship and milestones to complete, learn and apply learnings on your Ideas

Do I need to have a proper business model before registering?

        No, You can register with just an Idea or even if you just want an experience and learn new things.

Is having a team must to register in the bootcamp?

        No, you can participate alone or you can have maximum 3 member team to participate in this Bootcamp.

Can people not from IIESTS participate?

        Yeah sure, but atleast 1 member should be from IIEST Shibpur.

What are the perks?

        You'll get to know how to start from scratch and go to market ready products and services through mentorship and technical facilities, EDC will provide certificates to all the participants.

Will we get funding in this event?

        Yeah, you'll get the chance to pitch your ideas to the Investors.
